About

The E-learning system is a system that enables students to learn computer programming languages through online.It also allows the students to view the various courses. It is developed because of the covid pandemic. Therefore, the project aimed at solving such problems by designing a web-based system that will enable the students to learn the new languages. Using E-learning system makes things easier for you, as it helps plan the training activities calendar which you can share with your learners, trainer, and co-administrators. By doing this you can maintain and improve your training process. It also helps in getting trainer reports, which is a time consuming process when done manually. This is a fact that each individual learns in a unique manner. If you apply different learning approaches, they increase your success rate. E-learning systems help in communication and balance the whole system. In this digital world the working system has totally changed. Today every employee is engaged with a laptop or desktop, and with the help of E-learning system you make the training future ready. It becomes easy to offer centralized source of learning to multiple users and the training, performance, and content can be accessed from the same source. Another benefit of using an E-learning system is that you can enhance performance through tracking and reporting tools.
